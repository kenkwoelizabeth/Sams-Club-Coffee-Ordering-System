WELCOME TO MY COFFEE SHOP INVENTORY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The Coffee Inventory Management System is a web-based application designed to streamline the operations of coffee shops and cafes. It focuses on managing menu items, orders, and supplier information to ensure smooth day-to-day operations, maintain inventory levels, and establish strong supplier relationships.
